Privacy Policy

Effective: 23 June 2026

Overview

Basis ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. Basis is a finance education app — we collect only what is necessary to provide the service and improve your learning experience.

Information We Collect

Account data (optional): If you create an account, we collect your email address and, if you sign in via Apple or Google, a unique identifier from that provider. Creating an account is optional — the app works in full guest mode with progress saved locally on your device.

Progress data: Your XP, streak, completed lessons, and daily goals are stored locally on your device. If you create an account, this data is also synced to our servers (Supabase) so you can access your progress across devices.

Notification preferences: If you enable streak reminders, we store the reminder time you choose. Notifications are sent via Expo's notification infrastructure and are processed locally on your device.

Usage data: We do not currently run analytics beyond what is necessary to identify bugs and crashes. We do not sell your data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Basis app
  • Sync your learning progress across devices (if you have an account)
  • Send streak reminder notifications (only if you enable them)
  • Process your subscription if you subscribe to Career Accelerator

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to third parties, or share it except as described below.

Third-Party Services

Supabase: We use Supabase for authentication and cloud progress sync. Your email and progress data are stored on Supabase's infrastructure. See supabase.com/privacy for their privacy policy.

Apple / Google Sign In: If you use Apple or Google to sign in, those providers share a unique identifier and optionally your email address with us. Their privacy policies govern their handling of your data.

Expo: The app is built with Expo, which may collect device and crash data for debugging purposes. See expo.dev/privacy.

RevenueCat: Subscription management is handled via RevenueCat. Their privacy policy applies to subscription transactions.

Data Retention

Guest users: All data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

Account users: Your account data and progress remain on our servers until you request deletion. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the address below.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (including notification permissions)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: privacy@basisapp.co

Children's Privacy

Basis is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as we add new features. We will notify you of significant changes via the app. Continued use of Basis after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at:

privacy@basisapp.co
